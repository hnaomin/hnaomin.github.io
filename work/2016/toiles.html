"Past, Present and Future"

Classic toile collection with a twist. Each print represents past, present and future with tongue-in-cheek humor.
Designed for upholstery or wallcovering and targeted for those who like to make a statement in their space.
All motifs were hand-drawn before being digitally edited on Adobe Photoshop, and then screen-printed on varieties of cotton, polyester and velveteen fabrics.
